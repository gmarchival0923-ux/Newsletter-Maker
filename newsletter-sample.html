Lead Story

5 Market Forces Making Data-Driven HR a Top Priority for Buyers

- By Parul Gupta, Social media and community manager at HR.com

Decision-making based on data is no longer a new trend in HR; it is quickly turning into a must-have for businesses.

Facing rising pressure to boost workforce productivity, enhance retention rates, skills forecasting, and provide the best experience for employees, HR executives are forced to make more strategic choices using hard evidence instead of gut feeling. The result is the emergence of a major market opportunity for HR tech providers, consultants, and service firms.

Hot Marketing Takes

The latest B2B marketing landscape is shifting, and recent podcasts are highlighting the need for commercial impact and stronger agency partnerships. The B2B Marketing Podcast recently released three essential episodes. Episode 218 features Harry Davies from Sage, who discusses the truth about agency relationships, covering red flags and how to avoid drift. Episode 215 explores the tension between creativity and commercial impact with the “UK Agency of the Year,” Omne. Additionally, Episode 213 dives into "Best Answer Marketing" with Lee Odden of TopRank Marketing, emphasizing trust, buying groups, and the impact of AI-driven content overload. These discussions offer practical frameworks for marketers seeking to drive stronger results in an increasingly complex market.

In addition to these strategic discussions, practical application is essential. The Exit Five Live session showcases “B2B Ad Campaigns That Actually Worked,” featuring five demand gen marketers sharing their real-world results. Presenters break down specific campaigns, ranging from an $8K influencer campaign that generated $700K in pipeline to effective multi-channel signal-based targeting and conference plays that achieved significant ROI. These sessions provide actionable, data-backed examples that marketing professionals can implement to optimize their own demand generation strategies without relying solely on theoretical advice.
